Jelena Boyarkina

Watches and Jewellery Advisor

Creative and dynamic, every day Joke Gilissen organises her customer appointments down to the smallest detail, while infusing a touch of magic to create an unforgettable experience. Since her arrival in 2009, she has occupied various roles within Maison De Greef. With her valuable experience of products, customers and service, she is now equipped to excel in the art of welcoming and advising customers.

I have been perfecting my knowledge of the House's values, codes and sales circuits for ten years. Internally, I interact with all areas of the business: design, marketing, merchandising, events and after-sales. Externally, I regularly liaise with partner companies. This vast network of collaborations allows me to exchange best practices and cultivate diverse knowledge.

I enjoy guiding and advising our customers in the very prestigious and highly innovative sectors of watchmaking and jewellery. It is a real privilege to present creations made from noble materials with refined designs.

In my opinion, the strength of our House lies in our creativity, in the dedication of our teams and in our ability to create personalised and exclusive experiences for each of our customers.
